**An interactive sustainability tool that estimates the carbon emissions, energy use, and water footprint of everyday AI prompts in real time.**

As AI becomes part of daily life, its environmental impact often remains hidden. This project makes that invisible cost easier to understand by turning AI usage into clear estimates, relatable comparisons, and practical sustainability tips.

## 📊 What It Estimates

- Energy used in kilowatt-hours
- Carbon emissions in kilograms of CO₂e
- Water usage in liters
- Relative impact level based on task type and model profile

## 🌐 Why This Matters

AI tools require electricity, data centers, and computing power to generate responses. While a single prompt may seem small, repeated use across millions of users can create a meaningful environmental footprint.

By helping users see the estimated impact of their AI activity, this tracker encourages more informed and sustainable technology habits.

## ✨ Features

- Real-time AI footprint estimates
- Model profile selection
- Task type selection
- Electricity grid region selection
- Carbon, energy, and water calculations
- Relatable comparisons, such as phone charges, laptop hours, and miles driven
- Session history tracking
- Sustainability tips based on impact level
- Clean, beginner-friendly Streamlit interface

## 🧮 How the Estimate Works

The app uses simplified prototype assumptions to estimate AI usage impact:

- Energy per prompt × number of prompts = total energy used
- Total energy × regional carbon factor = estimated CO₂ emissions
- Total energy × water factor = estimated water usage

These estimates are designed to improve awareness and support testing, not provide exact scientific measurements.

## 🎯 Mission

The mission of this project is to make the environmental impact of AI more visible, understandable, and actionable. By helping users connect everyday AI prompts to real-world energy, carbon, and water use, the tracker encourages more responsible and sustainable AI habits.
